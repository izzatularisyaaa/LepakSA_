HELP CENTRE - Let’s Get Started with LepakSA



1. Choose user.

2. Key in your preferred destination in the search box.

In the search box, it will display the related destination based on the keyword prompted.

3. Choose your preferred destination in the list of destination appeared.

4. Info of the chosen destination will be displayed. You can read the info and details provided.

5. Click for location to bring you to any GPS app available.

Also, you may visit their social media (if any) for more info and don’t forget to give rate!
